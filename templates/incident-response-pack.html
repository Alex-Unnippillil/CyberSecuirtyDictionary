Mini Glossary

Incident Response
Coordinated steps for addressing and managing the aftermath of a security breach or attack.
Containment
Actions taken to limit the scope and impact of a security incident.
Eradication
Steps to remove the cause of the incident and ensure it does not recur.
Recovery
Process of restoring systems to normal operation and verifying they are functioning correctly.

Runbooks

Phishing Email Response

  1. Report suspicious email to security team.
  2. Isolate affected user account and device.
  3. Analyze email headers and payload.
  4. Remove malicious content and reset credentials.
  5. Inform users and update phishing filters.

Ransomware Outbreak

  1. Disconnect infected systems from the network.
  2. Notify incident response team and relevant stakeholders.
  3. Identify strain and determine scope of encryption.
  4. Restore systems from clean backups.
  5. Conduct lessons learned and strengthen defenses.
