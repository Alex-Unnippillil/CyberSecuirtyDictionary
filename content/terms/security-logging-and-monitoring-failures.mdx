OWASP identifies Security Logging and Monitoring Failures as weaknesses where insufficient logging or monitoring delays detection of attacks and incidents.

Source: [OWASP Top 10 - Security Logging and Monitoring Failures](https://owasp.org/Top10/A09_2021-Security_Logging_and_Monitoring_Failures/)
