---
title: Broken Access Control
slug: broken-access-control
summary: >-
  Broken Access Control occurs when users can act outside their intended permissions,
  enabling unauthorized access to resources or operations.
---

OWASP describes Broken Access Control as a common vulnerability that occurs when users can act outside of their intended permissions, allowing unauthorized access to resources or operations.

Source: [OWASP Top 10 - Broken Access Control](https://owasp.org/Top10/A01_2021-Broken_Access_Control/)
