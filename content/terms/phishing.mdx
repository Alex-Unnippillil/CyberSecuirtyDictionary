# Phishing

Phishing is a social engineering technique that uses fraudulent communication to trick targets into revealing sensitive information or performing unsafe actions.
The MITRE ATT&CK framework catalogs phishing as [Technique T1566](https://attack.mitre.org/techniques/T1566/).
The NIST Computer Security Resource Center describes phishing in its glossary [entry](https://csrc.nist.gov/glossary/term/phishing).
