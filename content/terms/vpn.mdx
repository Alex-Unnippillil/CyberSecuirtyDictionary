A Virtual Private Network (VPN) creates an encrypted tunnel over the public internet, allowing users to securely connect to a private network.

Source: [Cloudflare Learning: What is a VPN?](https://www.cloudflare.com/learning/network-layer/what-is-a-vpn/)
