OWASP explains that Identification and Authentication Failures, formerly known as Broken Authentication, result from incorrect implementation of authentication mechanisms, enabling attackers to compromise credentials or session tokens.

Source: [OWASP Top 10 - Identification and Authentication Failures](https://owasp.org/Top10/A07_2021-Identification_and_Authentication_Failures/)
