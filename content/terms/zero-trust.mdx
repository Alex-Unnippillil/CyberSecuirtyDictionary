Zero Trust is a security model that assumes no implicit trust, requiring strict identity verification for every user and device attempting to access resources, regardless of their location.

Source: [NIST SP 800-207, Zero Trust Architecture](https://csrc.nist.gov/publications/detail/sp/800-207/final)
