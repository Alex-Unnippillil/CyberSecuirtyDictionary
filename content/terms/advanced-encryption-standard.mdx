The Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) is a symmetric block cipher established by NIST to protect classified information and widely adopted for securing data.

Source: [NIST FIPS 197, Advanced Encryption Standard (AES)](https://csrc.nist.gov/publications/detail/fips/197/final)
