Term Lifecycle Policy

Creation

New terms start in the draft stage. Draft entries include a proposed definition and sources for review.

Review

Draft terms are reviewed for accuracy, clarity, and sourcing. Once approved, a term's stage is set to reviewed.

Deprecation

Reviewed terms may later become outdated. Such entries move to the deprecated stage but remain visible with a note about their status.

Archival

Deprecated terms that are no longer relevant are archived. Archived entries are kept for historical reference but are hidden from normal browsing.
